Driving instructions from I-40:

Take exit 335, turn westbound on Historic Route 66. We are ¾-mile straight ahead on the left side with easy in and out access.

Driving instructions from U.S. Route 54:

Take the U.S. 54 bypass highway when you get close to Tucumcari. At the stop light at U.S. 54 and U.S. Route 66, turn left (eastbound) onto Historic Route 66. We are ½-mile straight ahead on the right side with easy in and out access.
