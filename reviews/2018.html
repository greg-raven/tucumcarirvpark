We got our kicks on Route 66!

What a great RVing experience! We were headed from Albuquerque to Wellington, TX, for Nomad Fest and wanted to have a little stopover in Tucumcari to check out some of the Route 66 nostalgia. We got on Campendium and looked for RV parks and this one came up. We then googled it and read the reviews. People were raving about the new ownership and all of the positive updating and upgrades being done to the park.

I called from the road and they were almost full but had a space for us. We were not going to be arriving until about 8 p.m. and they said they would be there for us. We showed up and were greeted by a very friendly host (one of the owners) who checked us in and lead us to our spot, in the dark with the wind blowing and the temp about 40 degrees. We set up our rig and settled in.

The next morning we discovered the dog park which our crew loved and visited the office to join Passport America and pay for the next two nights. This place is neat! It is right on Historic Route 66, close to town, clean, well kept, has shower and laundry facilities, nice level and large spaces, and even horse pens if you are horse people. It is a quiet park with full hookups and reasonable rates.

We will definitely be coming back to stay here again!

— REPURPOSEDreams, TX, October 2018 (via Campendium)
